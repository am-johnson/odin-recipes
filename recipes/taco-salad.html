Taco Salad

A more flavorful take on traditional stuffed bell peppers.

This recipe makes 4 servings.

Feel free to substitute your favorite vegetarian or vegan alternatives to make this dish work for you.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Lean Ground Turky Meat
  • 1 can Fire Roasted Tomatoes
  • 8 cups Lettuce (Any type you like -- I use romaine)
  • 1 Package of Taco Seasoning
  • 2 cups Cooked Rice (Any type you like - I like to use brown rice)
  • 1 can Black Beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can Corn
  • 1 (15-16 oz) Jar of Salsa
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Sour Cream
  • Sliced Avocado

Steps

    Cook ground turkey and mix in taco seasoning.
    In a bowl add the turkey, lettuce, fire roasted tomatoes, cooked rice, black beans, and corn.
    Serve topped with sour cream, shredded chese, sliced avocado, crushed tortilla chips, and 2 tbsp of salsa.
